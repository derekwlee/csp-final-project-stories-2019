Stories:

I actually got allergies after playing in some grass as a kid. I was a stupid little kid, my mom let me outside. When I was 1 or 2 I was in the backyard with my younger brother Kiddus playing. And while I was playing in the grass, I was wearing short sleeves and shorts while rolling around. When I got inside my mother looked at the hives and rashes I got on my arms and legs. Around the same time I got an asthma attack.... love that! A little two year old unable to breathe! Wow!
